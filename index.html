About

My name is Damian and I design stuff :) Good design solves a problem, and good art evokes emotion. And at the intersection you find an experience unlike no other. At the foundation of what I do lies a very simple mythos, foster a relationship between user and design. Humans are social creatures, and as such we crave connection, it brings us together, it brings us closer. Why design when you can nurture? Give your product the care it deserves, and give your users a connection unlike no other. For truly memorable experiences.
